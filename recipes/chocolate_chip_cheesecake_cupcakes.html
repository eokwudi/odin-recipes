Chocolate Chip Cheesecake Cupcakes

Description

Chocolate Chip Cheesecake Cupcakes sound like a gift from heaven. This delectable dessert combines two of the best things to ever exist: chocolate and cheesecake! Chocolate and cheesecake create the actual cupcake, while buttercream frosting and chocolate chips create the topping.

Portion sizes vary depending on the chef (that is you). The cupcakes can be frozen before frosting is added and cupcakes should be refrigerated if any remain. Milk can be substituted for heavy cream instead of the frosting but frosting may lack thickness.

Ingredients

  • Coffee
  • Buttermilk
  • Bittersweet Chocolate
  • Cocoa Powder
  • Cream Cheese

Steps

Cupcakes

  1. Pour hot brewed coffe over cocoa powder and chopped chocolate squares. Stir until the chocolate is melted
  2. Add the remaining ingredients and beat to combine. Scoop the batter into a lined cupcake pan. Bake for 18 to 20 minutes

Frosting

  1. Beat the butter and cream cheese, then add remaining ingredients. Fold in chocolate morsels.

Assemble

  1. Pipe cream cheese frosting onto cupcakes using frosting bags. Sprinkle more mini morsels on top and enjoy.
